Accommodations

In The Glorious Hotel we have world class facilities that suit your every needs!

  • Bedroom: The bedrooms come with king sized beds and a mosquito net.
  • Bathroom: The bathroom facilities are well maintained and come with a sink, a toiled, shower, and bathtub. The bathrooms also include complimentary toiletries.
  • Minifridge:A minifridge is included in the rooms but for every thing you take out you will be charged. The fridge is NOT inclusive of the fees.
  • Room Service: We include room service to satisfy our customer demand. Room service is also an additional fee.
